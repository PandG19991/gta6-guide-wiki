# Design System: Leonida Ledger

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

A premium guide-command desk for GTA 6 searchers: editorial, tactical, and readable. Density is medium-high because the user needs quick answers, but the page still breathes through strong gutters, open rows, source rails, and crisp dividers. The visual world is a sunlit paper-map desk, not a neon crime poster and not a SaaS dashboard.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- **Sun-Faded Canvas** (#F7F4EE) - Primary background.
- **Dossier Paper** (#FFFDF8) - Main content surface.
- **Asphalt Ink** (#161616) - Primary text and dark guide icons.
- **Weathered Graphite** (#3C3A36) - Secondary text.
- **Muted Ledger Gray** (#69655D) - Captions, metadata, and helper text.
- **Rule Line** (#D8D2C6) - Borders and table grid lines.
- **Marker Coral** (#EF4B3E) - Single primary accent for CTAs, active nav, routes, and status emphasis.
- **Map Teal** (#317B76) - Functional secondary detail for map/evidence accents only.

## 3. Typography Rules

- **Display:** Satoshi or Aptos Display fallback, heavy uppercase, tight but not negative tracking.
- **Body:** Satoshi or Aptos fallback, relaxed line-height, max-width around 65 characters.
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono or Cascadia Mono for dates, source priority, and technical metadata.
- **Banned:** Inter-first styling, generic purple-blue gradients, official GTA logo imitation, and browser-default control typography.

## 4. Component Stylings

- **Buttons:** Rectangular, tactile, high-contrast. Primary is Asphalt Ink and turns Marker Coral on hover.
- **Guide Rows:** Dense horizontal records with a dark icon slab, answer summary, quick bullets, and a confidence badge.
- **Confidence Rail:** Left-hand evidence legend, using icons/initials and short definitions.
- **Tables:** Source-led, visible row rules, mono dates, no nested cards.
- **Panels:** Used only for focused explanation blocks, with square edges and paper fill.

## 5. Layout Principles

- Hero is asymmetric: large title and CTA on the left, original map/dossier artwork on the right.
- The homepage shows the next section immediately via the fact strip and guide board.
- Avoid equal repeated feature cards where possible; vary widths and density.
- Mobile collapses to one column with nav scroll and stable touch targets.
- Do not make the page feel official Rockstar-owned; the brand mark must stay original.

## 6. Motion & Interaction

- Use restrained motion only: hover color, active button press, filter updates.
- Avoid custom cursors, parallax, constant loops, or effects that distract from fast search answers.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences.

## 7. Anti-Patterns

- No official GTA, Rockstar, or Take-Two logos.
- No leaked or copyrighted screenshots as default site assets.
- No exact-match official-looking domain identity.
- No fake metrics, fake cheat codes, fake mission pages, or AI-generated filler.
- No purple/neon glow, bokeh/orb decoration, or generic SaaS dashboard styling.
- No ad placements that crowd navigation, buttons, or under-construction pages.
