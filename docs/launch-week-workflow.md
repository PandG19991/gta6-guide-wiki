## Publication gate

- The result answers a real player question.
- The method works on the recorded version and platform.
- Claims distinguish tested behavior from official statements.
- Images or video have an approved rights record and useful alt text.
- Story spoilers are labeled before the spoiler appears.
- Related guides and the relevant database page link both ways.
- The guide has a clear update trigger for patches or platform differences.
